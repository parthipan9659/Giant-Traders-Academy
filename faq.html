💬General Questions
What is Giant Traders Academy?
+
Giant Traders Academy is a premium trading education program based in Tamil Nadu, India. We teach rule-based index options buying strategies for Nifty, Bank Nifty, Fin Nifty, Sensex, and Midcap — with a focus on capital protection and trader discipline.
Is this about algo trading or automation?
+
No. This is a 100% manual trading education program. We teach you the strategy, logic, and discipline to manually trade index options yourself — no software, no bots, no automation required.
Who is this program designed for?
+
This program is designed for serious traders — beginners who want to start with the right foundation, and experienced traders who want to replace indicator-based approaches with a structured, rule-based system.
Do I need prior trading experience?
+
No prior experience is required, but you should have basic familiarity with what options are and how the stock market functions. Absolute beginners may need to spend a few hours understanding options basics before enrolling.
🎓The Program
Which indices and strategies are covered?
+
The program covers all 5 major Indian indices — Nifty, Bank Nifty, Fin Nifty, Sensex, and Midcap Index — each with its own specific options buying strategy, entry logic, exit framework, and risk management approach.
Is this indicator-based or rule-based?
+
100% rule-based. We do not use RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, or any other indicators. Every entry and exit decision is determined by a defined rule — making the system objective, repeatable, and teachable.
How long is the course?
+
The core video content spans 6 comprehensive modules. Most students complete the core videos within 2–3 weeks. However, real mastery comes from applying the strategies live over 60–90 days, supported by our Google Sheet updates and mentorship sessions.
Is the course in Tamil or English?
+
The course is primarily taught in Tamil, with key concepts also explained in English. Our student community uses both languages freely.

📈Trading Questions
How much capital do I need to start trading?
+
We recommend starting with a minimum of ₹1–2 lakh dedicated trading capital. The exact amount depends on your risk appetite and the index you choose to trade. This is discussed in detail in the Capital Protection Model module.
Can I trade with a small account?
+
Yes, but with certain limitations. Some index strategies require higher premium budgets. We'll guide you on which strategies are most suitable based on your available capital during the program.
How much time per day does trading require?
+
The strategies are designed for focused, rule-based decision making — not screen-watching. Most students dedicate 1–2 hours per trading day to market monitoring and trade execution. Specific time requirements are covered in the program.
Does this guarantee profits?
+
No. Trading involves risk and no legitimate program can guarantee profits. What we guarantee is teaching you a structured, proven approach to index options buying — with built-in capital protection and discipline training. Your results depend on how consistently you apply the system.
