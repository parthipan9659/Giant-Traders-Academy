WHY WE EXIST

Tired of Courses That Don't Actually Teach Trading

When we started trading, we went through dozens of courses, workshops, and YouTube channels. Most of them taught indicators — RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, support and resistance lines. And we lost money following every single one of them.

The problem wasn't effort. The problem was the framework. Indicator-based trading requires subjective interpretation — which means every trader using the "same strategy" is actually doing something completely different.

After years of real market experience, we discovered something that changed everything: numerical, rule-based systems. Systems where every entry, every exit, every decision is defined by a rule that removes subjectivity entirely.

We built Giant Traders Academy to share exactly that — a structured, teachable system that works for Nifty, Bank Nifty, Fin Nifty, Sensex, and Midcap — backed by capital protection and trader discipline training.
